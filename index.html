Who We Are

About the Committee

🕉️

Our Mission

Jalpai Ganesh Puja Committee is dedicated to organizing Ganesh Puja with unwavering devotion, unity, and discipline — bringing together the entire community in a spirit of joy and reverence for Ganpati Bappa. Every year we create a celebration that is grand in scale and pure in spirit.

🏡

Our Community

Rooted in Jalpai, Bagnan, Howrah — our community is celebrated for its unity, rich cultural heritage, and the warmth of its people. Together we honor tradition, forge lasting bonds, and carry forward the legacy of our ancestors with pride and devotion.
